Let's stop the spread of coronavirus!

A list of covid prevention guidelines are provided here to help us keep ourselves and those around us safe

Some facts about the covid19 virus:

It spreads primarily through droplets of saliva or discharge from the nose when an infected person coughs or sneezes

COVID-19 can be severe, and has caused millions of deaths around the world as well as lasting health problems in some who have survived the illness.





Most common symptoms

Fever.
Dry cough.
Tiredness.
Aches and pains

Less common symptoms

Sore throat.
Diarrhoea.
Conjuctivitis.
Headache.
Loss of taste and smell.
A rash on the skin, or discolouration of fingers or toes.

Serious symptoms

Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath.
Chest pain or pressure.
Loss of speech or movement

Prevention measures

Protecting yourself


Maintain physical distance when in public spaces.
Refrain from smoking and other activities that weaken the lungs.
Avoid touching your face
Clean your hands regularly with soap and water, or using an alcohol-based sanitizer.
Maintain a healthy weight and stay as physically fit as you can

Protecting others


Stay home if you're feeling unwell.
Cover your mouth when coughing or sneezing.
Wear a mask when distance cannot be maintained
Keep rooms ventilated when with visitors at home
